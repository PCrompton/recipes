Lemon Chicken 

marinade:
1 cup lemon juice
1/3 cup olive oil
1/8 cup honey (or squeeze bottle for 4-5 seconds)
1 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Paprika
½ tsp. Pepper

Put ingredients in a pan.  Heat until honey is melted and ingredients are mixed well.  Place one 5lb package of frozen chicken tenders in a baking pan. Pour marinade over chicken and cover with foil.  Bake at 350 for about 1 hour.

thickening
1 ½ Tblsp. Corn starch

Put a little water in a mixing/ shaking jar.  Add the corn starch.  Shake vigorously.  Add mixture to  chicken when chicken is nearly done. Stir mixture into marinade.  Put in oven for a few more minutes until sauce is thickened.
